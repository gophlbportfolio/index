  • The tile war (alfa version, October 2019):
    A board game for two (or a very bored one) in which you will have to defeat your enemy.
    Everytime you refresh the page you will get a random grid with random placement of chips and non playable tiles.
    You decide your strategy: merge your chips, destroy the enemy and absorb its energy if your chip is stronger, or be a kamikaze and sacrifice your chip just to weaken the enemy.

    Known issues:
    • Sometimes free tiles are not recognized as free, so the movement is not allowed.
    • Sometimes the merge (or attack) doesn't happen and the chip just rest above the other.


    Technology used:
    • Angular 8 + rxjs
    • html5
    • css3
